# Roles and Business Purpose

| Role | Business Purpose |
|------|------------------|
| System Administrator | Owns the overall KPI Management System, ensuring organizational structure, users, roles, and system settings are correctly maintained and governed. |
| Department Manager | A department lead responsible for defining performance objectives, creating KPIs, assigning them to employees, and reviewing submitted measurements. |
| Employee | An individual contributor responsible for executing assigned objectives, submitting KPI measurements, and monitoring personal performance progress. |
| Executive | A senior decision-maker responsible for monitoring organizational performance through dashboards, reports, and strategic analytics without participating in operational data entry. |

# Notes

1. **Department Managers can only access data within their own department.**
   They are not permitted to view or modify KPIs, measurements, or employees belonging to other departments.

2. **Managers can only update or delete KPIs they created (Own Only).**
   This prevents department managers from modifying strategic objectives owned by another manager.

3. **Employees can only view KPIs assigned to them and can only create, update, or delete their own measurement submissions.**
   They cannot modify KPI definitions.

4. **Measurement approval is restricted to Department Managers.**
   This establishes a formal review workflow where submitted results must be validated before becoming official.

5. **Executives have organization-wide read-only visibility.**
   They can access dashboards, reports, departments, KPIs, measurements, and notifications but cannot modify operational data.

6. **System Administrators have unrestricted access to all entities.**
   Their responsibility is governance, maintenance, and platform administration rather than business performance management.

7. **Notifications are generated by the system or managers and are primarily consumed by users.**
   Employees and executives can read notifications addressed to them but cannot create or modify notification records.

8. **User profile updates are limited to the user's own account.**
   Role assignment and user lifecycle management remain under System Administrator control.

9. **Department Managers can view employees within their department only.**
   This is represented as **Own Only** under User access.

10. **Approval/Reject operations exist only for Measurements.**
    KPI definitions, departments, users, and notifications do not require an approval workflow in the current business model.
